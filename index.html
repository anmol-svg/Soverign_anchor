I. The Sovereign Bet — The Soil

We do not pick stocks in a vacuum. We first select the ecosystem. We go into a growing country where the population is striving for relevance. If the country rises, the tide lifts all boats.

II. The Enterprise — The Organism

Once the soil is chosen, we look for the strongest organism:

  • Growing Company: It must be expanding its utility.
  • The Leader: A group of people is only as effective as the person they follow. We look for management that treats capital as a sacred trust.
  • Historical Precedence: We avoid the unproven. We look for patterns that have worked for centuries.
